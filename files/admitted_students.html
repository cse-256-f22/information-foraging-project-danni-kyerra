Offer Acceptance Procedure

  1. Once accepted into the school, fill out the Developmental History Form (only needs to be done once).
  2. Complete all the forms in the "Getting Ready For School" Section.
  3. Pay 1 month tuition as deposit. More information in the 'TUITION DEPOSIT' section.

ORIENTATION

Orientation Day: This visiting day is usually the day after Labor Day and gives the child and parent(s) an opportunity to visit the classroom and meet the teachers and classmates. This visit is one hour. It will allow time for the child to enjoy free play and also gives parent(s) a brief overview of what to expect during the school year. The next day, a child will attend school on his/her own (either Wednesday or Thursday, see orientation schedule). The class is divided in half, attending on either Wednesday or Thursday. This schedule allows the children and teachers to interact with a small group before the entire class attends on Friday.


TUITION DEPOSIT

Upon acceptance, a nonrefundable deposit of one month’s tuition is due. This deposit will be applied to tuition for the following May. It is $500 for the half-day session, and $1105 for the full-day session. It can be paid online here or by mail.
